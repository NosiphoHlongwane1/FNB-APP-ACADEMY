Gemsbok

The Gemsbok (Oryx gazella) is a large antelope native to arid regions of Southern Africa. Known for its striking appearance, the gemsbok has long, straight horns and bold black-and-white facial markings.

Gemsboks are well adapted to desert life. They can survive extreme heat and go for long periods without water by getting moisture from the plants they eat.

They are strong and agile, capable of defending themselves against predators like lions using their sharp horns. Both males and females possess horns, which can reach up to 85 cm in length.
