Who we are:
We are a local café that specializes in coffee, latte, and matcha with a variety of pastries.
Established in 2010 in Braamfontein, Johannesburg, we later expanded to Parkmore. We now offer an online delivery system so customers can enjoy their favorite coffee and pastry from anywhere in South Africa.
